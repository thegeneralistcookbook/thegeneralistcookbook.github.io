Adobe Acrobat ConnectorIcons

2021-07-06
  • I have seen this a lot on Citrix app servers, not so much RDS boxes for some reason, the user’s profiles will fill up because of:

C:\Users\xyz\AppData\LocalLow\Adobe\Acrobat\DC\ConnectorIcons\xyz.bmp

  • Still not sure what causes this but they are just cached icons of documents

  • You can safely delete them for all users using the following powershell script:

Get-ChildItem C:\Users | Foreach-Object { Remove-Item -Path "C:\Users\$_\AppData\LocalLow\Adobe\Acrobat\DC\ConnectorIcons\*.bmp" -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue -Force -Recurse }
  • Can even make it a scheduled task - who cares
